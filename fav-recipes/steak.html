Steak

To make this simple but delicious dish is easy, We need the following

  • Meat
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Black pepper
  • Rosemary

How to cook it

  1. Season the meat with salt and black pepper
  2. Put the meat on a high temperature pan
  3. Add the butter with the rosemary
  4. After few minutes when its ready let it rest for 3 minutes outside the pan before cutting it
  5. Cut, salt, and Enjoy!
